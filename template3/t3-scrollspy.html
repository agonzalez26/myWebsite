About MexiKeto

Sebastian Hernandez founded MexiKeto in 2018 in hopes to inspire others with the non-traditional mexican keto meals. He wanted to introduce new methods for creating traditional meals into healthier alternatives and keeping the spicy authenticity to his cultural dishes.

Opening up the new location in Lilburn, Georgia has been a success with new loyal customers enjoying the new essence of mexican food.
